# Question Log

## Rule
把問題集中放這裡，避免散落在聊天、文件和腦中。

每個問題都盡量標記：

- priority
- owner
- current guess
- unblock condition

## Open Questions
- Question:
  Priority:
  Asked to:
  Current guess:
  Why it matters:
  Next action:

## Answered Questions
- Question:
  Answer:
  Source:
  Follow-up:

## Repeated Confusions
如果同類問題重複出現，表示它可能值得升級成 playbook 或 note。
